Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our chatbot service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our chatbot:

  • Conversation Data: The messages you send to the chatbot and the responses you receive.
  • Session Information: Information about your session, including timestamps, device information, and IP address.
  • User Information: If you provide personal information during conversations, such as your name, email address, or other contact details.
  • Files and Attachments: Any files or documents you upload or download through the chatbot.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our chatbot service
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active Conversations: Up to 180 days from the last activity
  • Archived Conversations: Up to 365 days from when the conversation ended
  • User Account Information: As long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter

After these periods, your data will be automatically deleted or anonymized.

5. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and other applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data that we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure data storage practices

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help us provide the chatbot service. These services may have access to your personal data, but only to the extent necessary to perform their functions. We ensure that all third-party processors comply with applicable data protection laws.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience with our chatbot. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the chatbot or by other means. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
